The Concept

Liverpool's streets
are an art gallery.

Frames is a Flutter mobile app that turns the city's historic doors into portals for discovering local artists. Walk to a door, scan its QR code, and unlock an artist profile — complete with biography, artwork, and a collectible card to add to your sticker book.

"Every door in Liverpool has a story. Frames makes sure you can hear it."

— Frames, CASA0015 UCL 2026

How It Works

Four steps to discovery.

01
Open the Map

A radar-style map shows 9 hidden doors across Liverpool's neighbourhoods. Orange markers are found, blue are waiting.

02
Walk to a Door

Navigate to a real door in Liverpool. The radar pulses gold when you're within 100 metres of an undiscovered door.

03
Scan the QR Code

Point your camera at the QR code on the door. Sparks fly, haptic feedback fires, and the door unlocks.

04
Collect the Artist

An artist card flips to reveal their biography, artwork, and rarity rating. Share it, collect them all, earn badges.

Gamification

More than a map.

Frames turns Liverpool into a living game — with rewards for exploration, collection, and discovery.

⭐

Rarity System

Doors are rated Common, Rare or Legendary — each worth different points and harder to find.

🏆

Badges & Achievements

9 unlockable badges with Liverpool landmark illustrations — from First Steps to Liverpool Legend.

🚪

Sticker Collection

Every found door becomes a coloured sticker in your neighbourhood book. Complete areas to unlock completion badges.

📍

Proximity Alert

The radar turns gold and pulses faster as you approach an undiscovered door within 100 metres.

🥇

First Finder

Be the first person to scan a door and claim a special First Finder badge for that location.

📅

Door of the Week

One featured door highlighted in gold on the map each week — a changing reason to keep exploring.

Built with Flutter.
Powered by Firebase.
