## Introduction to Application

Frames is a Flutter geocaching mobile application that turns Liverpool's 
historic doors into a city-wide art trail. Users physically walk to 
GPS-located doors across Liverpool's neighbourhoods, scan QR codes to 
unlock profiles of local Liverpool artists, and build a personal 
collection of door stickers. The app features a radar-style proximity 
map that pulses when users are within 100 metres of an undiscovered door, 
a card flip interaction revealing artist biographies and artwork, a 
rarity system with Common, Rare and Legendary doors worth different 
points, 9 unlockable badges, a live leaderboard, daily streak tracking, 
and a door nomination system for community growth. The app is built in 
Flutter and Dart with a Firebase backend including Firestore, 
Authentication, and Storage. It integrates Google Maps, the Geocoding 
API, and the device GPS and camera sensors as core interaction points. 
The landing page is live at 
https://yussr19.github.io/casa0015-mobile-assessment-frames and the 
Android release build is available at 
https://github.com/yussr19/casa0015-mobile-assessment-frames/releases/tag/v1.0.0
